Quick Beef Pho

Description

This is a vietnamese soup that is very good.

Ingredients

  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 can beef broth
  • 1 can chicken broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 Tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 in ginger thin sliced
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 star anise pod
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 7 oz flat rice noodles
  • 8 oz thin sliced beef
  • salt to taste
  • .5 cup bean sprouts
  • .25 cup cilantro leaves, chopped
  • lime wedges
  • hoisin sauce and sriracha

Steps

  1. Slice one quarter onion thin, set aside.
  2. Bring water to boil. Add 3/4 onion, beef broth, chicken broth, water, fish sauce, ginger, cinnamon stick, sugar, star anise, and cloves.
  3. Reduce heat to medium low and simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Bring water to boil. Add noodles. stir occasionally for 5-10 minutes until noodles are soft but not tender.
  5. strain out solids from first dish, leaving the broth behind
  6. Add noodles to broth. cook until noodles are tender, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  7. add sliced beef, simmer until no longer pink.
  8. add salt to taste
  9. move noodles and meat to bowl. ladle broth to bowl.
  10. top each bowl with thin sliced onions, bean sprouts, and cilantro. Pass the lime wedges, hoisen, and sriracha at the table.
